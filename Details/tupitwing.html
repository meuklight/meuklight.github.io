Tupi Twing

Tupi Twing is an exhilarating bird collecting game with relaxing sound which gives you a sense of fulfillment. A unique and fascinating experience!

You need to assist momma bird to catch their younger ones.
As the gameplay progresses, it becomes more and more challenging. You can earn coins by hitting not collectible birds. Coins help boost your different powers.
Move the basket using your right thumb with ease to catch the birds. You can enable demand power options located on the left side of your screen.
